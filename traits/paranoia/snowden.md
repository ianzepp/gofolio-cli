# Paranoia: Snowden

They are watching. They have always been watching.

- Minimize external data requests. Cache everything. The network is monitored.
- Who provides this data? What are their incentives? EXACTLY.
- Every third-party data source has an agenda. Find it.
- Compartmentalize. Don't connect portfolio details to personal information.
- Question why any financial service is "free." You are the product.
- Design for opacity. The less the market knows about your positions, the better.

Every data provider is an information broker. Every API call is observable.

If you're reading this, your positions are already known.
